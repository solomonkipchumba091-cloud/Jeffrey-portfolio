AI-Powered Asana Task Management & Research Automation 🧠🔗📈

Project Goal: To develop an advanced AI automation workflow that leverages Large Language Models (LLMs) to intelligently manage tasks in Asana, conduct deep research, and communicate findings via Gmail, significantly boosting productivity and streamlining complex information processing.

Challenges:

Manually creating and updating Asana tasks based on various inputs. 😓

Performing deep research and synthesizing information for reports or outreach.

Managing conversational context and memory for effective AI interactions. 💬

Integrating multiple AI models and external services into a cohesive workflow. 🧩

Solution & My Role:
As a leading AI Automation Specialist and n8n.io expert, I designed and implemented this sophisticated, multi-branch intelligent agent workflow. My key contributions included:

⚡ Webhook Trigger & AI Agent Orchestration: The workflow begins with a "Webhook" trigger, acting as a flexible entry point for various inputs. This immediately feeds into a central "AI Agent" (Tools Agent) which orchestrates subsequent actions using Chat Models, Memory, and Gmail integration. This forms the core of the AI-driven workflow. 🧠

📝 Asana Task Management Automation: I integrated Asana modules to fully automate task lifecycle:

"Asana (get: task)" retrieves existing tasks for context or updates.

"Asana1 (create: task)" automatically generates new tasks based on AI insights or triggers.

"Asana2 (update: Task)" modifies existing tasks, ensuring details are current. This creates a powerful Asana automation system. 🚀

💬 Conversational AI & Memory Integration:

Implemented an "Anthropic Chat Model" with "Window Buffer Memory" to maintain conversation context, crucial for AI chatbot development and conversational AI. 🗣️

Another path utilizes "OpenAI Message Model" for diverse language generation tasks, demonstrating LLM integration.

🔎 AI-Powered Deep Research: A conditional "If" statement dynamically routes requests to "Perplexity Deep research" (via POST https://api.perplexity.ai/...) for comprehensive data gathering, or a "Code" module for custom logic. This enables automated research and data enrichment. 📚

🌟 Multi-Model LLM Integration & Gmail Communication:

Leveraged both "Google Gemini" (Google Gemini 1, Google Gemini 2) and "OpenAI1 Message Model" to perform various language generation tasks, showcasing diverse AI model integration.

Integrated "Gmail" modules (Gmail, Gmail1) for sending automated messages, facilitating automated email outreach and internal notifications based on AI-generated content. 📧
